Documents Required for Aadhar Card

  • Identity Proof (Document must consist photograph) - PAN card, Driving license, Secondary or Senior School mark sheet/ School leaving certificate having photograph, Government-issued identity card/ certificate.
  • Address Proof - Electricity/water/gas bill (last 3 months), Bank / Post Office passbook, rent/ lease/ leave & license agreement.
  • Both Identity & Address Proof - Ration card, Voter identity card, Government-issued identity card/ certificate having address, and Indian Passport.

Documents Required for PAN Card

  • Identity Proof - Ration card, Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Driving license, Passport.
  • Address Proof - Ration card, Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Driving license, Passport, Spouse’s Passport, Post Office Passbook.
  • Date of Birth Proof - Matriculation certificate, Birth Certificate, Aadhaar card, Voter ID, Driving license, Passport.

Fees for PAN applications submitted Online through paperless modes (e-KYC & e-Sign / e-Sign scanned based / DSC scanned based): Dispatch of physical PAN Card in India (Communication address is Indian address) – Rs. 101

Time period for pan card to be dispatched - 2 weeks
