Frequently Asked Questions

What is Indivest ?
Indivest is a transactional and an educational platform, helping you create wealth! Our motto is to help you invest, and achieve financial freedom, by empowering you to make your own informed decisions.
Why invest with Indivest ?
Indivest educates you about why and how to invest through simple videos to help you to make informed decisions easily, understands your profile and educates you about your financial health. It also empowers you to choose from different investment options like Mutual Funds, Deposits and Stocks.
What’s in store for you ?
You will be provided with handholding for the end-to-end process through animated videos and snippets to educate you! We promise you won’t get bored. Want a test drive before investing actual money?? Indivest allows you to create your very own customized virtual portfolio, where you can earn virtual money and invest it. You can also share your portfolio with your friends and gift your Indivest coins to them!
Will Indivest help me in achieving my financial goals and objectives?
Yes, Indivest will definitely help you in achieving your financial goals and objectives through one of our IFAs (independent financial advisors).
Are there any account opening charges at Indivest ?
Indivest doesn’t charge you for opening an account with us. It is absolutely free of cost!
Can I create my own watchlist of funds on Indivest ?
Yes, you can create your own watchlist of funds on Indivest through the Virtual Portfolio.
Can non-resident Indians (NRIs) invest in mutual funds through Indivest ?
No, currently NRIs cannot invest in mutual funds through Indivest.
Do I need an account in a bank to invest in mutual funds through Indivest ?
Yes, it is mandatory to have a bank account to be able to invest in mutual funds through Indivest.
How do referrals work ?
You can earn coins by sharing your referral link with your family and friends. When someone signs up on our platform through your referral link, you earn 10,000 Indivest coins! While sharing your referral link, you can also gift coins to your family & friends to welcome them onboard.
What is the minimum amount you can invest in mutual funds through SIP on Indivest ?
You can start investing in SIP with as low as INR 100 on Indivest.
Does Indivest charge me for using the platform ?
No, Indivest does not charge you for using the platform. It is free of cost.
Does Indivest have its own investment products ?
No, Indivest does not have its own investment products. Hence, it has no vested interest in the recommendations it gives to you.
How much money can I earn by referring another person ?
There is no limit to the number of Indivest coins you can earn by referring others. Share the referral link, spread the knowledge, and keep earning!
Is Indivest associated with any bank or AMC?
No, Indivest is not associated with any banks or AMCs. It is an independent entity.
How long does it take to reach a customer care executive via call on Indivest ?
Once you raise a ticket on Indivest, a customer care executive will get in touch with you at the earliest.
What is Indivest Virtual Portfolio ?
The most striking feature of Indivest is its virtual portfolio. Here, you can actually earn while you learn. You can create your personalized list of securities and invest in them with all the Indivest coins that you earn. You can earn coins through interesting ways like scratching a card, spinning the wheel and referring your friends.
How long does it take to open an Indivest account ?
The procedure to open an account with Indivest take less than 15 minutes.
What documents are required to open an account online ?
The following documents are required to open an online Indivest account: 1. Pan card 2. Voter ID/ passport/ driver's license 3. Cancelled cheque
What are the charges for opening an account at Indivest ?
Zero. There are no charges to open an Indivest account. It is absolutely free of cost.
What are the ways in which you can earn Indivest coins ?
Indivest coins can be earned by sharing your referral link with your family & friends and investing the Indivest coins earned to make more money!
